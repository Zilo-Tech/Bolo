Job Description

Farmers Needed - Earn 2000-2500 CFA/Day Join our thriving agricultural cooperative in Bamenda. Work the land, grow valuable crops, and earn a competitive daily wage. Ideal for experienced farmers passionate about contributing to the local food supply. Apply now!

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Farming Experience: Minimum 2-3 years of hands-on experience in cultivating crops and managing agricultural operations.
  • Knowledge of Farming Techniques: Understanding of best practices in soil preparation, planting, irrigation, pest control, and harvesting.
  • Physical Strength and Stamina: Ability to perform physically demanding farm work, including lifting heavy loads, operating machinery, and working outdoors for extended periods.
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Aptitude for identifying and addressing challenges that may arise during the farming process.
  • Attention to Detail: Diligence in monitoring crop growth, maintaining farm equipment, and ensuring quality control.

Education + Experience

  • Educational Background: Completion of primary or secondary education with a focus on agriculture or a related field.
  • Vocational Training: Participation in agricultural training programs or workshops to enhance farming skills and knowledge.
  • Relevant Experience: 2-3 years of practical experience working on a farm or in a similar agricultural setting.
